"What are the most credible cases involving triangular craft in the US after 2000?"
High Confidence

The Meridian corpus contains 1,247 cases involving triangular craft descriptions within the United States from 2000 to present. Of these, 14 are classified Tier 1 and 89 are classified Tier 2. The highest-scoring cases share several common descriptors: silent or near-silent operation, dark or black coloration, lights at each vertex, and very large estimated wingspan (often exceeding 200 feet).

The three highest-credibility triangular craft cases post-2000 are the Tinley Park Triangle (2004, Score: 78), the Stephenville Additional Witness Group (2008, Score: 72), and the Hudson Valley Continuation Events (2003, Score: 68). The Tinley Park case is notable for having multiple independent video recordings from different locations that were later geometrically corroborated.

A concentration of triangular craft reports appears in the Illinois–Indiana corridor, with 23 Tier 2+ cases between 2001 and 2015. This cluster is examined in detail in the Geographic Clustering Report v2.1.
