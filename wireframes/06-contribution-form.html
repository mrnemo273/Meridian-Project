How Your Submission Is Scored

Every submission is AI-screened for internal consistency, specificity, and cross-referenced against the existing corpus. Your credibility tier (1–4) is determined by factors including witness count, corroborating evidence, professional credentials, and detail specificity. This process is transparent — you'll see your preliminary tier on the review page.
