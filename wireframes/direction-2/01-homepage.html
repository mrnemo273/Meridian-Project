What is Meridian?

Think of it as a search engine for the world's most comprehensive UAP evidence record — powered by AI that finds patterns no human researcher could.

We've aggregated every major public source: government archives, congressional testimony, declassified documents, and 150,000+ civilian reports. Our AI analyzes them for patterns, scores every case for credibility, and publishes findings — including when the evidence doesn't support a conclusion.

No conspiracy theories. No sensationalism. Just the evidence, rigorously analyzed, with full methodology transparency on everything we publish.
